Historic and Heritage Court Houses in New South Wales

Starting at $75.00 AUD

Travelling to every corner of the State, Lachlan has photographed architectural features of over 400 operating and former Court House Buildings and they are all showcased in this book.

Included in this unique photographic collection, the reader will discover:
  • • 115 Operating Heritage registered Court Houses built prior to WWII
  • • 37 Heritage registered Former Court Houses now operating as Museums
  • • 92 Former Court Houses / Court Rooms now used as Police Stations
  • • 48 Former Court Houses now used as Privately Owned Residences
  • • 38 Former Court Houses used for Community and Commercial Purposes
  • • 54 Contemporary Operating Court Houses built after WWII
  • • 20 Additional Buildings directivity related in some way to Court Jurisdictions
  • • Numerous Design variations of Court House Coat of Arms
  • • Pictorial samples of External Decorative Stonework and Ornamental Freatures
